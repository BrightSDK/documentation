title: "Bright Video"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Bright video?">
    Bright Video is a new way for YouTube creators to create additional revenue from their videos, by featuring it on SmartTV platforms. In a nutshell, your YouTube videos will automatically be available inside apps on most SmartTV platforms (Android, Apple TV, LG, Samsung, etc.).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="But YouTube exists. Why is this a good idea?">
    YouTube will never be replaced. This is a way to capture more audiences; many smart TV users look for apps on the TVs App Store, but at this point TV App Store do not include many apps… Try searching for “Guitar TV” or “ASMR TV”; there are literally no such apps. This makes it easy to stand out and be discovered. Make sure you enjoy the first movie vantage.!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why should I join Bright Video?">
    Why not? It is a great way to extend your reach to Smart TV App users. Of course, you will make money from videos viewed on Bright Video! And lastly, it is 100% free and doesn't require any work from you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I have a YouTube channel. Do I need to do anything? Upload a video somewhere?">
    **No.** We will download your videos and meta data from YouTube automatically you do not need to change anything in your work flows.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use this to promote my YouTube channel?">
    **Yes.** The app will include the QR codes to allow users to instantly subscribe to your YouTube channel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do you make money in order to pay me?">
    Bright Video monetizes your videos using [Bright SDK](https://docs.bright-sdk.com/help-center/general-introduction#what-is-bright-sdk-for-developers).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Bright Data and what is Bright SDK?">
    Bright SDK is a monetization method that is already used by hundreds of app developers. Bright Video leverages Bright SDK in order to generate revenues that are shared with you. You can find more information about Bright SDK here [https://bright-sdk.com/](https://bright-sdk.com/). Keep in mind that the information is presented mostly for app developers. In the case of bright video, we develop the actual Smart TV apps and include Bright SDK inside the apps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I maximize my earnings?">
    Your app is live? Here are a few things you can do to maximize your earnings- [read how](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lY0m9jlNhhtmbusLChTeJY-eqzy_HdaUqzBL52TDHX8/edit).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Am I licensing anything to you?">
    **No!** It goes without saying: you retain full ownership of your videos to use any way you wish.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does this increase my earnings?">
    We pay you as much as 5X your existing YouTube RPM for views inside the app. Payment done via your preferred method - PayPal or Wire transfer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Bright Video make money to pay Video publishers?">
    Bright Video apps are powered by **Bright SDK**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to add my app links to the descriptions of my entire existing video catalog or just new uploads?">
    To maximize your revenue it’s best to add to old videos as well. If not all, then at least the most popular ones.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What kind of apps are going live?">
    Multiple apps, by topic. For example: ASMR TV, Guitar TV, Chess TV, Surfing TV, Comedy TV, etc. Every channel will include contents from multiple YouTube creators in the vertical.

    We are now also creating personal apps! It's FREE! interested? [Signup here](https://bright-sdk.com/brightvideo).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have my own private app for my channel?">
    **Yes!** And it’s free. You don’t need to do anything special all other terms here apply to your own app. To prepare your app, we need to know things like app name, icons, images etc.

    [Please signup here.](https://bright-sdk.com/brightvideo)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much can I expect to earn?">
    Bright Video pays you \$10 NET RPM per 1,000 videos. In other words, every video view earns you one cent. Earnings are proportional to the number of video views your content will receive.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is the content on the smart TV apps refreshed?">
    Typically, videos that are uploaded to YouTube will be available inside the smart TV apps within the hour.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I make the most revenue from Bright Video?">
    We suggest you promote the app your video is featured in, in your YouTube video, descriptions, and social accounts.

    [Here are a few tips and how to’s](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lY0m9jlNhhtmbusLChTeJY-eqzy_HdaUqzBL52TDHX8/edit?usp=sharing)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a commitment?">
    **No.**

    You can stop anytime if you do, we will simply remove your content from the smart TV apps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can I contact if I have more questions?">
    Email us at [sdk@bright-sdk.com](mailto:sdk@bright-sdk.com)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to change anything in how I work?">
    **No**, just keep making great videos. The Videos are synced from your YouTube channel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an agreement?">
    **Yes**, a simple one. There is no risk. The agreement is nonexclusive, you keep ownership of your content, and you can terminate anytime for any reason, we will just remove your content from the apps the same day. [Review our contract here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/161hqYO1d2-_YGh_g5QtlD0Ta_IeKvGrjY1FYsz6xbng/edit).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What views is payment paid based on: YouTube or my personalized Smart TV app?">
    Payment is calculated based on views in your personal app so it is _on top_ of your YouTube revenue.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will you include all videos in my channel?">
    Yes by default, but if you want you can exclude some videos, based on keywords.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does Bright Video do to promote my app once it is created?">
    Following the app's launch, we allocate marketing budgets and produce the creative assets to actively promote your content on platforms that support promotional efforts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
