title: "Submit Integration Review"
---

1. **Pre-Submission Requirements**

The Bright Data SDK compliance team will verify:

- SDK integration UX and functionality.
- Consent screen implementation.
- Opt-out mechanism.
- User value proposition.

2. **Submission Process**
   1. **Run Self-Check (takes few minutes) -** Complete the self-check verification to identify potential issues before submission:
      - Navigate to the [<u>Bright SDK Dashboard</u>](https://bright-sdk.com/cp/apps) and click your appID.
      - Scroll down to "Implementation self-check", click "Get started".
      - Install the app on the test device and follow the instructions.
      - If the self-check passed successfully, submit it for review!
   2. **Submit for review via Dashboard**
      - Navigate to the [<u>Bright SDK Dashboard</u>](https://bright-sdk.com/cp/apps)<u>.</u>
      - Select your app ID.
      - Click "Submit for Review".
      - Complete the compliance checklist to validate adherence to guidelines.
      - Upload your package.

   3. **Store submission -** You will be notified by email once your app is approved for release. The app status in the dashboard will also be changed to "Approved".
      We've prepared these guides to help you through the store review:
      - [<u>LG</u>](https://docs.bright-sdk.com/technical-implementation/how-to-upload-apps-to-the-lg-store)
      - [<u>Samsung</u>](https://docs.bright-sdk.com/integration-guides/how-to-upload-apps-to-the-samsung-store)
      Review Resources:
      - Watch our [<u>guided tour video</u>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kQCmca0FHyw) for critical submission tips.
      - Review our [<u>quality standards</u>](https://bright-sdk.com/blog/monetization-optimization/ensuring-excellence-bright-sdks-guidelines-for-excellent-apps) for faster approval.
