Bright SDK is a monetization solution developed by Bright Data that enables app developers to generate revenue by allowing their users to contribute idle device resources to Bright Data's trusted network.

This documentation is your complete guide to integrating, implementing, and managing Bright SDK across all supported platforms. Whether you're building for iOS, Windows, Samsung Tizen, or LG WebOS, you'll find everything you need here — from step-by-step integration guides and platform-specific requirements, to compliance guidelines and app submission best practices.

Here you'll find:

📦 Platform-specific SDK implementation guides.

🔒 Compliance, privacy & security guidelines.

❓ Frequently asked questions for developers and end-users.

✅ App review & submission best practices.

💡 Tips for maximizing revenue across your apps.
