Bright SDK is a new way to monetize your apps - working quietly in the background, without ever disturbing your users. It's fully GDPR/CCPA compliant and easy to integrate.

This documentation is your complete guide to integrating, implementing, and managing Bright SDK across all supported platforms. Whether you're building for iOS, Windows, Samsung Tizen, or LG WebOS, you'll find everything you need here — from step-by-step integration guides and platform-specific requirements, to compliance guidelines and app submission best practices.
